` The Golkonda
A Visit to Golkonda Fort

Posted date: 2nd April 2023

Would like to share my experience on one of the historical place golkonda. Where visited with my family members(my wife and daughter). The first site view of the fort seems bigger. There were two gates In and Out gates for tourist. And also we need to buy the tickets where the prices are around 25/- for Indian Citizens and for foreigners it is 250/- around. I don't rememnber the exact price. But, to guid on the history and on the way there were guides and security gaurds available.

Highlights

Enterance gate. The Echo.

Enterance Gate

Golconda fort Entry Gate also called as “FATEH DARWAZA”. This Entry Gate Consists of Iron pierce with a sharp point which will protects from enemies attacks the fort with Elephants then it hits into its head and immediately hot oil was poured by the Knights at the time of ruling of Shah’s .Exactly opposite to the Main Entrance a Artillery was there to kill the enemies without attacking the fort.

The Echo

After Crossing the Entry Gate, there is a diamond shaped architecture was there in the top of the echo system where exactly in a particular point of place if any one clapped it will reflects the sound and it can be heard till the top of the kings Darbar. At the time of Shah’s Ruling if any person will come to the fort this Echo System helps to inform to the King immediately.
